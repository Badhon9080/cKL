About Us

For over twenty years, Coldco Logistics has been bringing the most innovative and effective solutions in cold chain transportation, logistics, and warehousing for customers across industries. We are committed to perpetual advancement and consistent service.

About Our CoMpaNy

Over the past 20 years, Coldco Logistics has provided logistics solutions to more than 500 local, regional, national, and international customers across various industries.

Our comprehensive 3PL, warehousing, and transportation network allows us to cater to several industries that have very strict and specific temperature requirements when it comes to the storage and transport of their products. We are a logistics company focused on creating efficiencies within our customer’s cold chains in various industries through our innovative solutions, industry expertise, and widespread infrastructure network.
